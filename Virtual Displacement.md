#### **Virtual Displacement** $\delta u^i$
A **virtual displacement** $\delta u^i(\mathbf{x})$ is an imaginary, infinitesimal, arbitrary [[Displacement]] [[Field]] imposed on the body. It is *not* the actual displacement but a small variation from the current configuration. It must be:
*   **Infinitesimal:** Small enough that the equations of geometry remain linear.
*   **Kinematically Admissible:** It must satisfy the internal compatibility conditions and the essential (geometric) boundary conditions. That is, $\delta u^i = 0$ on the part of the boundary $\partial \Omega_u$ where displacements are prescribed.
