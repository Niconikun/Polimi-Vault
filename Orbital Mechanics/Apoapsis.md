**Apoapsis** is the point in an [[Elliptical Orbit]] around a primary body where the orbiting object is at its **maximum distance** from the primary's [[Center of Mass]]. It is the most distant point of the [[Orbit]]. As well, it is the point in the [[Orbit]] with minimum [[Velocity]].
The **apoapsis distance**, denoted as **rₐ** or **Q**, is calculated as:  
$$rₐ = a(1 + e)$$ Where:
- **a** is the **[[Semi-Major Axis]]** of the ellipse.
- **e** is the **orbital [[Eccentricity]]**.
