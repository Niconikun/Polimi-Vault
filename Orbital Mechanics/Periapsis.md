**Periapsis** is the point in an [[Elliptical Orbit]] around a primary body where the orbiting object is at its **minimum distance** from the primary's [[Center of Mass]]. It is the closest approach of the [[Orbit]]. Not only that, but is the point in the orbit with maximum [[Velocity]].

The **periapsis distance**, denoted as **rₚ** or **q**, is calculated as:  
  $$rₚ = a(1 - e)$$
  Where:
- **a** is the **[[Semi-Major Axis]]** of the ellipse.
- **e** is the **orbital [[Eccentricity]]**.
